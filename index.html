The Musicians Clinic is a 2-day program to help high school students in the greater Seattle area refine their musicianship by working with the graduate students from the School of Music and any interested undergraduates who are interested in sharing their advanced skills in music. It is a great opportunity for high school students to gain a deeper appreciation of music and increase their breadth of musical knowledge.

Featured Sessions

Masterclasses taught by graduate students from the School of Music and advanced undergraduates who are interested in sharing their skills in music.

FAQ

How many students are we expecting at this event?

This is the first time we are doing anything like this, so our estimate is anywhere from 50-80 students

What do you want from the graduate students?

Mainly lead master classes for each instrument.

If grad students participate in this, what are they committing to?

Approximately 6-9 hours over the course of 2 days. A great sense of reward from helping spread the knowledge of music. Gain experience in working with high school students.

Why would these high school students want to come all the way to campus for this program?

This is unlike any band/jazz/choral festival they have attended because it is much more intricate in that it helps students refine the skills on their instruments first before focusing on how to play together in an ensemble. They could begin establishing a network of music professionals before they even start college. Revamp their interest in music from a new setting.

Will this information be sent over email?

Yes. Si Si will email Professor Salzman and ask him to forward her email to members of the Wind Ensemble

Who should we contact if we have any questions?

Si Si syc25@uw.edu or Brian bhuynh37@gmail.com

About Kappa Kappa Psi

Kappa Kappa Psi is a National Honorary Band Service Fraternity for college and university band members. It was founded in 1919 and primarily operates as a society providing service, leadership opportunities, and social bonds for band members.
